CHARACTER ORB

The Character Orb can be obtained by killing the unique skeleton, Prince Albrecht, in the Den of Evil on Hatred difficulty. He drops 4 of them, for an easier multiplayer experience. This charm is there to help newcomers and speed up the start for the veterans. Read it carefully and do not use it at level 120 as it will reduce your Life to 1 point.
