KNOWN BUGS

My mercenary stands there and doesn't do anything!
This is a bug with mercenary AI in Diablo II. If you walk, the mercenary will also walk, and this occasionally causes it to freeze up. To cure this, jump into a town portal.

My mercenary is slow and does no damage!
Due to various bugs with mercenary auras in Diablo II, reequipping items and dying are excuses for your mercenary to slack off. You are not supposed to see how things like this work under the hood, but it does mean that the mercenary may sink into a depression and require a game restart to get back into the battle mindset!
