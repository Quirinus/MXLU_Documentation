F.A.Q.

  • Q: What does this thingy do?
    A: It allows you to plan how many points you can put into each skill without running out of skill points when all those +maxskill-items start pouring in.
  • Q: I don't want to click the buttons 20 times until my skills are maxed!
    A: You can Shift-Click the + and - buttons.
  • Q: When will the most recent version be supported?
    A: Usually after the official release. I will not support beta-versions, pre-releases or similar. Since I don't play MXL any more, I probably won't notice new releases right away - send me a PM on the forums if that happens. (Note: Tub doesn't post on the new forum, so you can report everything here.)
  • Q: Can I plan a character with legacy items/charms?
    A: Not with this planner. That's intentional.
  • Q: Can I save my character?
    A: This is a planner, not a cheating tool. It will not create a character for you to play with.
    However, you can save your skill-plan, so you can look at it later, show it to your friends or link it in your guide. Just enter a name, hit the save button, then follow the instructions.
  • Q: Can I use this tool offline?
    A: Sure. Hit "File" -> "Save Page As" to make a local copy. You can also download Full documentation in HTML format which includes this calculator.
  • Q: How much damage will my skills do?
    A: I don't know, and my planner can't tell you.
  • Q: Some Skill-Icons are wrong!
    A: Some Skill-Icons changed in newer patches. This calculator will always use the most current icon for each skill.
  • Q: I noticed a bug or have a suggestion.
    A: Go ahead, post in this thread. I'll make sure to fix any serious bugs.
  • Q: I cannot put Points into "Light and Shadow".
    A: That's how the game worked before Omega 002. Pick any of the Light or Dark Level 1 skills first.
  • Q: Can I offer this tool on my homepage or similar?
    A: Not without my permission. Contact me first. (Note by Quirinus: Tub's not responding to emails currenty, so...)
  • Q: Who should I thank for this nifty tool?
    A: Brother Laz of course, because he created the mod and the skill icons. :p
    I (Tub) just spent a couple of hours to hack a little 1000-line script together, hoping that others might find it as useful as I do. Quirinus updated it for all the Ultimative versions.
