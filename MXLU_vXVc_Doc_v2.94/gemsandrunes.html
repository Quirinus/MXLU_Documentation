Secret Elemental Runes
On Destruction difficulty, in the Unformed Land uberlevel, you can come by the Elemental Stones and Unknown Skulls 1. They all have the same chance to drop.

Unknown Skull 1

You can cube them to get the Elemental Runes and Kabraxis' Stone rune:

Elemental Stone + Unknown Skull 1 → Elemental Rune
Elemental Runes (all 6 different) + Unknown Skull 1 → Kabraxis' Stone rune

See THIS PAGE for more information about the Unformed Land uberlevel.
