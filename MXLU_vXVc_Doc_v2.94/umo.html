Unique Mystic Orbs

As normal Mystic Orbs, Unique Mystic Orb can be applied to proper items, but they add +10 Required Level to the item instead of +2 Required Level. They cannot be bought, however, and must be found. Unlike normal ones, Unique Mystic Orbs each have a different name - making them easy to spot on the ground and tell them apart from each other and normal orbs.

Any non-ethereal equippable item + Unique Mystic Orb → add orb bonus and +10 required level

You can only apply up to 15 of the same Unique Mystic Orb to your item.
Any (Unique) Mystic Orb effects applied on a runeword are lost upon unsocketing, but the effects applied to the base item stay.

Warning! The level requirement penalty is applied after taking into account both the base item reqired level and the level of any socket fillers. This means for example if you have a belt with required level 20, add one unique orb (level penalty +10) and socket a Rha rune (required level 100), you get a belt with required level 100+10 = 110. For that reason, to avoid going over required level 120 on accident, always add the socket fillers before adding orbs.

On Destruction difficulty, Heroic Boss Bull Prince Rodeo has 2% chance to drop an Unique Mystic Orb on death. They are also slightly more likely to drop from monsters on Hatred difficulty because of the reduced variety and amount of item drops compared to higher difficulties. Higher player number or setting can increase this chance as well, but only with monsters that have a chance to not drop anything (usually mostly weak monsters and/or on Hatred difficulty).

Oskills
If an item has a skill bonus with no character class requirement (eg. +3-6 to Summon Shadows), then the bonus is an 'oskill', giving this skill to any class for free.
