PREFIXES AND SUFFIXES LIST

This is a list of random rare affixes that can spawn on magic, rare and crafted items, listed with the required conditions. All you need to know to deduce what affixes can spawn on a particular item is the item quality level and item level (not to be confused with required level). Then you can use the included affix level calculators and the filter to determine how many and which affixes the item gets. This list is for MXL:U XIII.

Quality level
An item's quality level is usually a good indication of it's base strength. It only depends on the item type and tier. For sacred items it's 71 to 90, for jewels/rings/amulets it's 1, for arrow/bolt quivers it's 0 and for large/medium/small charms it's 1/14/28.

See THIS PAGE for a list of item quality levels (qlvl).

Item level
This number is usually a good indication of the strength and level of the area/monster that it dropped from. It can be different even for items of the same type and tier, but it can't be seen without 3rd party tools (like PlugY). This is NOT the item's required level.

If an item is gained trough a cube recipe, its output item level is capped between 1 and 99, but some cube recipes can benefit from and use an input item level that is higher than 99.

Item level depends on the monster/map level when dropping, character level when gambling/cubing, and difficulty & character level when shopping. You can make sure it's 99 by finding it in specific hard areas (after and including Terror – Act 4: The Chaos Sanctuary, and some uberquests before), gambling for it at level 120 or re-rolling it as a part of the Act 2 Dragon's Eggs challenge on Destruction.

See THIS PAGE for more information about the Dragon's Eggs challenge.

What affixes will a rare item get?
A rare item will get 3-6 random rare affixes (3-4 for jewels), with an equal chance for any number. Each affix has a 1/2 chance to be a prefix or a suffix, and there can be at most 3 prefixes and 3 suffixes.

Required level
Rare item required level is usually equal to the required level of the rare affix with the highest affix level or the required level of the base item, whichever is higher. There are exceptions though, mostly for the high affix level affixes.

What affixes will an item get when crafting?
The resulting crafted item spawns with 0-3 preset crafting affixes depending on luck and shrine type used (fixed 1 or 2 preset crafting affixes for jewels), and 1-4 extra random rare affixes, based on the item level:

Item level 1-30: 40% chance of 1 affix and 20% chance each of 2, 3 or 4 affixes.
Item level 31-50: 60% chance of 2 affixes and 20% chance each of 3 or 4 affixes.
Item level 51-70: 80% chance of 3 affixes and 20% chance of 4 affixes.
Item level 71+: 100% chance of 4 affixes.

Crafted items can have at most 3 prefixes and 3 suffixes chosen from the random rare affix pool. Each random rare affix has a 1/2 chance to be a prefix or a suffix.

Required level
Crafted item required level is whichever is higher – item's required level or:

[required level of the rare affix with the highest affix level] + 10 + 3 * [number of random rare affixes]

Especially at lower levels, this formula may create items with a required level above the level of the character that crafted them. This is a feature of crafting and occurs in classic LoD as well.
