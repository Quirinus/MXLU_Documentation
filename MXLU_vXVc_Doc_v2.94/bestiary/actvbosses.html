    Strategy: Facing the truth, Baal won’t be as hard as you could expect him to be.

    Right when you get into the Worldstone Chamber you’ll be surrounded by Visions of Destruction, yeah. But they actually have lousy HP, so if you have enough defense and pot fast enough you should be able of tanking them for the few seconds killing them will take.

    Once that’s done, it’s pretty straight forward.

    -Move slowly, so that only one Vision sees you at a time and get rid of them, then lure Baal to the already free side of the room and proceed to stab his eye with a fork repeatedly. He moves very fast even if he’s slowed AND will keep you perma-slowed, so you possibly won’t be able of ranging him too easily. Get extra def. to make up for the forced melee range.

    -Summoners don’t have a too hard enemy here, too. Their mobs will soon get rid of the first wave of Visions. Then moving slowly and perhaps summoning a lonely monster to lure them in tiny groups will do as well.
