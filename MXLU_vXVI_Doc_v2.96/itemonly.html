ITEM ONLY SKILLS

Aside from the regular character skills, Median XL: Ultimative features a variety of item-only skills. Some are so powerful and devastating that the human mind cannot learn or comprehend them. Others were abandoned in favour of new and stronger magic, but are still useful for adventurers looking for a solution to a particular problem.

Some of these can be cast much like regular skills when you equip the item that grants the skill. Others are triggered by items. Either way, these skills enable you to create a character like no other!

See THIS PAGE for a complete list of oskills and charged oskills on items (no descriptions).

Spell scaling
Item-only elemental spells gain a damage bonus from energy, just like regular elemental spells.

NOTE: Some Item-only skills are not listed yet on this page.
