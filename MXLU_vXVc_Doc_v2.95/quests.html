QUEST REFERENCE LIST

This is a short reference list for all the useful uberquests, quests, challenges and various other things.
They are ordered by difficulty and location. Hover the mouse over under-dotted text for more information.

Keep this in mind:

  • Horadric Amulet and Staff of Kings can be found in Claw Viper Temple level 2.
  • The complete Khalim's Will can be found by killing the council in Travincal.
  • To keep the bonus from each Scroll of Resistances, cube them with the Sunstone of the Elements.
  • Most of the uberquest rewards are charms. Some of them can be upgraded with the Trophy from the same boss.
  • If the charm has a fraction next to its name, it's the chance that it drops. Trophies always have 2% chance to drop.
  • To do summoning uberquests and challenges, you need the Dark Summoning skill to summon creatures.
    You can get it from the Book of Summoning, which is obtained by buying and cubing:

    Meditation Candle x3 + Sacrifical Heart x3 → Book of Summoning
